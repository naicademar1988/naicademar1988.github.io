This article covers Ada Ehi biography and other facts about her. Ada Ogochukwu Ehi also as nee Ndukauba is a Nigerian born performing artist, recording artist, songwriter, and gospel musician. Her music singles and albums are widely acknowledged both locally in Nigeria and on the international scene.

Ada Ehi Biography – Ada Ehi Parents – Ada Ehi Siblings

Ada Ehi is the daughter of Mabel and Victor Ndukuaba and was born on the 18th of September in 1987. She originated from Imo State, Nigeria. She also has three brothers and all of them grew up listening to a wide range of gospel recording. She came into the music scene when she was 10 years old starring as the backup singer to the child star “Tosin Jegede”. After this time, she went on to study Chemical & Polymer Engineering at the Lagos State University.

Ada Ehi Music Career — Ada Ehi Biography

During her schooling, she joined Believer’s Loveworld Campus Fellowship and later Christ Embassy Choir where she grew steadily until she became a member of the Christ Embassy Presidential Choir. After this time, she moved on to Loveworld Records in the year 2009.

This was her leverage and mode of entry into the International scene. She is an active member of the music ministry in Christ Embassy and has then performed at many tours and programs hosted by the church in America, Europe, and many different African countries.

In November 2009, she released her first album titled “Undenied”. This was followed by a two-disc album in November 2013 and was titled “Lifted & So Fly”. Her third studio album was released in October 2017. It stayed on the top of music charts for several weeks and got the No.1 spot on iTunes on the same day of release.

Ada Ehi Husband

According to her, she met her husband in church during one of her rehearsal sessions while still in school. They shortly became friends and finally tied the knot in 2008 before joining the Loveworld Records the following year. Ada Ehi husband is Ehi Moses and the couple is blessed with two children.
