The Restaurant and Bakery in MineColonies buildings are an important step in fully automating your town. If you give the Restaurant or Bakery the ingredients to make food and teach them the recipes that they will need to make the food, they can craft food for you. The Bakery only makes baked items, such as a cake or pumpkin pie, while the Restaurant will make almost any other form of food. This is important because your villagers need food to continue working, and the Restaurant and Bakery provide an easy alternative to having to give them food manually.

Installation:

For Client(SSP):

1. Make sure you have Minecraft Forge Installed
2. Locate your .minecraft folder
3. Open up the mods folder.(If you do not have one, create a new folder and name it mods)
4. Drag the BakeryCraft 1.6.4 .zip into the mods folder.
5. Open minecraft and enjoy

For Server(SMP):

1. Make sure you have Bakery Craft(Client) installed.
2. Navigate to your server folder and open minecraft_server.jar with any unzipping program (winrar, 7zip)
3. Put all Minecraft forge contents in that jar and make sure you delete the META-INF folder in the minecraft.jar
4. Place BakeryCraft in the Server Mods file.
5. Run server using a .bat and enjoy
