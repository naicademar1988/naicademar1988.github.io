Most Acoustica programs have a four part registration code in the following format: 12345-12345-12345-12345 (Each part consists of five numbers or letters.)

Your software will require that you enter your registration ID and your registration code. Your registration ID will usually be the email address you ordered the software with.

For example, if your email is johnny@appleseeds.com and your registration code was RTU2W-G75KW-TRHEK-YRT72, you'd enter:

Registration ID: johnny@appleseeds.com
Registration Code: RTU2W-G75KW-TRHEK-YRT72

If you have a bundle, such as the Power Pack, Power Pack PRO or Musician's Bundle, your registration email will instruct you to download a separate program that will activate all programs within that bundle.
