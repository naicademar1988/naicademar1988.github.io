  1. Launch GarageBand on your iPhone/iPad.

  2. Go to My Songs.

  3. Make sure Browse is highlighted, then tap Select and choose your song.

  4. Click the Share icon in the lower left, then click Song.

  5. High Quality (192 kBit/s) should be fine, so hit Share.

  6. Scroll down to Save to Files (if you wish, you could also text, Airdrop, email, or save to Google Drive).

  7. Scroll down to Downloads (or wherever you prefer to save your file).

  8. Change your Song Title (be sure to include your name!).

  9. Click Done, then Save, then Done again.

  10. Now you can share your file with the world! When you click Upload from Google Forms, you should see your song in the folder where you saved it.

How To Share Midi Stems On Garageband

  1. Launch GarageBand on your computer/laptop.

  2. Click Share from the top menu, then Export Song to Disk…

  3. Choose your preferred Location for the file.

  4. Select MP3 and High Quality.

  5. Give it a title in the Save As field (be sure to include your name!).

  6. Click Export.

  7. Now you can share your file with the world! When you click Upload from Google Forms, you should see your song in the folder where you saved it.
